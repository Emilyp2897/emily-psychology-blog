Use this toolkit in high-pressure moments when you feel yourself tightening up.

1. Pause for one breath before the next play.
2. Relax one body point: jaw, shoulders, or hands.
3. Say your reset line: "One action. One moment."
4. Commit to one clear decision and execute it.
5. After the action, reset immediately and move to the next play.

Pressure does not have to control your performance. A repeatable reset routine helps you stay composed when it matters most.
